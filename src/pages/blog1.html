Secrets to the Perfect Burger

3 minutes
Aug 8, 2024

At Burgery, we take pride in serving up the perfect burger every time. But what exactly goes into crafting that mouthwatering masterpiece? We sat down with our head chef, Ba Hamid, to uncover the secrets behind our beloved burgers. Here’s what we learned:

1. The Meat Matters

The foundation of any great burger is the patty. Our chef insists on using a blend that’s 80% lean and 20% fat. “That fat content is crucial,” Ba karim explains. “It ensures the burger stays juicy and flavorful, even after cooking.” We also source only the highest quality beef, which makes a noticeable difference in taste.

2. Seasoning is Key

While many might think seasoning a burger is just about adding salt and pepper, there’s more to it. “Seasoning should be simple but effective,” Ba Abdellah says. He recommends generously seasoning the outside of the patty right before it hits the grill. This helps create that perfect crust while locking in all the juices.

3. The Perfect Sear

Getting that crispy, caramelized sear on the burger is what elevates it to the next level. We achieve this by cooking our patties on a flat-top grill at high heat. The flat surface ensures an even cook and that signature crust. “Never press down on the burger while it’s cooking,” Ba Rachid advises. “You’ll lose all those delicious juices.”

4. Layering for Flavor

The way you build your burger is just as important as the patty itself. Start with a toasted bun—this adds texture and helps prevent sogginess. Then, layer your toppings strategically. “I like to put lettuce and tomato on the bottom to create a barrier between the bun and the juicy patty,” Ba Hassan shares. “This keeps the bun from getting too soggy.”

5. The Final Touch: The Bun

A great burger is nothing without the right bun. We use fresh, locally-baked buns that are sturdy enough to hold all the toppings but soft enough to melt in your mouth. Lightly toasting the buns adds an extra layer of flavor and a nice crunch.
Next time you bite into one of our burgers, you’ll know exactly what makes it so special. And if you’re feeling inspired, try these tips at home for your own burger night—just don’t forget to invite us!
