FAQS

Questions? Here are answers

What are your burger options?

We offer a variety of burger options including classic beef burgers, chicken burgers, and plant-based options for vegetarians and vegans. Each burger comes with a choice of toppings, sauces, and sides to customize your meal.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we do! We have gluten-free buns and patties available, as well as delicious vegan burgers made from plant-based ingredients. Just let us know your dietary preference when ordering.

Can I customize my burger?

Absolutely! You can build your own burger by selecting your preferred patty, bun, toppings, and sauces. Whether you like it classic or fully loaded, we’ve got you covered.

Do you offer delivery or takeout?

Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout services. You can place your order online through our website or by phone. Delivery options may vary based on location.

What are your opening hours?

Our restaurant is open during the following hours:
  • Mon - Sat : 8:00 AM To 9:00 PM
  • Sunday : 9:00 AM To 1:00 AM
Please note that hours may vary during holidays, so be sure to check our website or give us a call for the latest updates.
