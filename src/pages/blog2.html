Beyond the Burger: Exploring Our Delicious Sides and Shakes

6 minutes
Jun 16, 2024

At Burgery, we’re known for our mouthwatering burgers, but our menu offers so much more than just that. Our sides and shakes are the perfect complements to your meal, turning a great burger into an unforgettable experience. Here’s a closer look at what makes our sides and shakes so irresistible.

1. Crispy, Golden Fries

No burger is complete without a side of fries, and ours are the best in town. We start with fresh potatoes, cut them to the perfect thickness, and fry them to a golden crisp. Each fry is seasoned to perfection, ensuring that every bite is crunchy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. For an extra treat, try our loaded fries topped with melted cheese, crispy bacon, and a drizzle of our special sauce.

2. Onion Rings with a Twist

Our onion rings are not your average side. We use thick-cut onions, coat them in a flavorful batter, and fry them until they’re golden brown. The result is a crunchy exterior that gives way to a tender, sweet onion inside. For those who like a little kick, we offer spicy onion rings, with just the right amount of heat.

3. Gourmet Shakes to Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth

Our shakes are the perfect way to end your meal—or start it! Made with premium ice cream, our shakes come in classic flavors like chocolate and vanilla, as well as more adventurous options like salted caramel and cookies and cream. Each shake is blended to perfection, thick and creamy, and topped with whipped cream. Want to make it even better? Add a shot of espresso or a splash of flavor syrup for a custom creation.

4. Pairing Suggestions

Looking for the perfect combo? Our chef recommends pairing our classic cheeseburger with a side of crispy fries and a chocolate shake for a timeless meal. If you’re in the mood for something a little different, try our spicy onion rings with our BBQ bacon burger and a salted caramel shake. The sweet and salty combination is simply unbeatable.
